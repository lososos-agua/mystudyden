# MyStudyDen Product Brief

## One-Line Definition

MyStudyDen helps students turn messy course materials into weekly study packets and an actionable course dashboard.

## Target User

- Undergraduate and graduate students taking multiple courses.
- Students juggling PDFs, slides, notes, LMS pages, NotebookLM, ChatGPT, and calendar deadlines.
- Students who need to know what to study next, what is due soon, and what they still do not understand.

## Product Thesis

The semester is the container. The study packet is the unit. Learning state is the differentiator.

## MVP Scope

Included:

- Manual semester workspace.
- Course creation.
- Pasted text, personal note, and URL/manual source capture.
- Mock study packet generation.
- Course dashboard.
- Semester dashboard.
- Learning state markers: understood, shaky, confused, important, exam-relevant, review later.
- AI Tutor Handoff prompt generation.

Deferred:

- Canvas/LMS integration.
- Full PDF ingestion.
- Account system.
- Sync.
- Payments.
- In-app live AI tutor chat.
- Full flashcard or Quizlet replacement.

## Core Flow

```text
Capture source
-> Select course
-> Choose intent: Organize, Study Guide, Quiz Me
-> Generate StudyPacket
-> Update dashboard, concepts, questions, and review queue
```
